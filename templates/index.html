Honda Box

Instructions

This site makes it easy to move pictures from your phone or computer to your car. It appropriately scales and crops any photo youprovide based on your model and makes it available in a folder only you can access. You can then download the photos directly to your car from this site over the internet (after some initial setup). For more information:
