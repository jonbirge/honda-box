Honda Box

Instructions

This site was created to make it easy to upload pictures of your own to your Honda to use as background images. It appropriately scales and crops any photo you provide, and then makes the new photo available on the internet so you can easily download it to your car using the build-in browser.

Didn't know your car had a web browser? It is probably disabled. If so, follow the instructions in the first video below to bring it back. Your car may already have access to the internet, either via the onboard cell connection or your phone. I recommend just enabling the browser (via the first video) and seeing if you have a connection. If you don't, then follow the instructions in the second video to connect to WiFi.

  • Enable browser in Honda
  • Honda WiFi connection

Please don't do any of this while driving, obviously. If you don't want to mess with any of this, you can just download the background files you create with this site and use a USB stick, per the instructions that came with your car.

Upload your own images

You can upload your own images. They will be processed to fit the size and shape of the screen on your Honda and will be stored on the server for you to download directly to your car later.
