Honda Box

Instructions

This site was created to make it easy to upload pictures of your own to your Honda to use as background images. It appropriately scales and crops any photo you upload provide based on your model and makes them available in a folder only you can access. You can then download the pictures as a backgrounds for your car's infotainment system via one of two ways:

Via USB stick

Most Hondas with Android-based infotainment systems allow the user upload background pictures via a USB stick. Download the processed images from this website to your computer and then copy to a USB stick. Consult your car's user manual for instructions on how to upload pictures from the USB stick to your car.

Via in-car browser

The easiest way may be to just download the pictures directly from the internet onto your car's system. After uploading your picture you'll be given a link where your background pictures can be downloaded directly from the car from the in-car web browser.

Didn't know your car had a web browser? Some Hondas come from the factory with a browser enabled. On more recent models Honda has disabled the browser. If you don't have access to a web browser, follow the instructions in the first video below to bring it back. Your car may already have access to the internet, either via the onboard cell connection or your phone. I recommend just enabling the browser (via the first video) and seeing if you have a connection. If you don't, then follow the instructions in the second video to connect to WiFi.
