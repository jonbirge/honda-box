Honda Box

Instructions

This site was created to make it easy to upload pictures of your own to your Honda to use as background images. It appropriately scales and crops any photo you provide, and then makes the new photo available on the internet so you can easily download it to your car using the build-in browser.

Didn't know your car had a web browser? To access the browser from your car, follow the instructions in one of the following videos:

Honda WiFi connection

Enable browser in Honda

Please don't do any of this while driving, obviously. To my knowledge, none of this will void your warranty, as it is all available via the existing software and does not involve any hacking of the system itself. (This is NOT the Honda Hack you may have heard of.) Nonetheless, if you don't want to mess with any of this, you can just download the files created on this site and use a USB stick, per the instructions that came with your car.

Upload images

You can upload your own images. They will be processed to fit the size and shape of the screen on your Honda and will be stored on the server for you to download directly to your car later.
