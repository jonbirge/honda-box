HondaBox

Instructions

This site makes it (somewhat) easy to move pictures from your phone or computer directly to your Honda to use as background pictures for the screen. It appropriately scales and crops any photo you provide based on your Honda model and makes it available on a webpage only you can access. Feedback

I originally created this website for my own use, but decided to make it available publicly in case others might find it useful. I'm happy to hear about any issues or suggestions for improvements, but can't guarantee I'll be able to respond. You can reach me at webmaster@hondabox.net.

Privacy Notice

Any photo uploaded can only be accessed from the internet by providing the PIN you provided during upload. However, the files are not stored in encrypted form, and anybody who gains or has system priviledges on the server will be able to see the pictures. In other words, don't upload anything illegal or sensitive.
